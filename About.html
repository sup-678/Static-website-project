About Us

We are a team of IT enthusiastic students on a mission to Improve the rural development in Nachod district located in Czech Republic.

Nachod is rich in history and natural attractions but still underrated or less popular. Our mission includes a genuine attempt to bring this place under spotlight so that more travelers who love Nature, adventure and history will get a chance to experience this hidden gem in northern Czech Republic. Our mission is not only just attracting more people to the district but also to help the district to develop its economy. There are several ways we are trying to strengthen the local economy by with the help of travelers who visit Nachod and would like to participate in the economy building process.

We as a team bringing you all the necessary information you need to know to spend your time and build awesome memorable in Nachod.But we are also presenting on the table not only the information but also the opportunity to be an investor or a business partner in Nachod. So you earn with Nachod and grow with Nachod.
