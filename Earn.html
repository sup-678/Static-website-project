Earn with Us

Description

Invest in unconventional accommodation

can invest in the unconventional accommodation provided in Nachod district to improve the facility and quality of the service. The type of accommodations includes Caravans, Wooden cabins, Container houses etc. By this you can secure lifetime free accommodation for a period of time according to the percentage of investment and small profit share.

Rent shop floors near major attractions

You can Rent the shop floors and Kiosks near major attractions and either have your own shop or partner up with the existing local business owner. By this you get access to the shop floors with frequent visit of tourists. In the case of partnership with local business owner to run the shop, the rent will be subsidized, and profit share will be according to the percentage investment.

Invest in IT Startup Hub

IT Startup Hub is a facility with infrastructure to accommodate emerging local IT startup in the district. You can be the owner and investor of your own IT start up or an investor in the existing, emerging startups. There must be an agreement on minimum number of local employees in your start up In the case of your own IT startup. Profit share is according to investment share or ownership.

Nature short film competition

This is an opportunity for creative future movie aspirants. You can present your short film at an open-air theater in the forest during the short film festival in Nachod. The major rule of the competition is that at least 10 min of the movie should be shot in Nachod and max time duration of the movie should be 30 min. Price money is waiting for the top 3 creations. Register now and pay the registration fees to secure your spot.

hotography competition in Nachod.

If you are a creative photographer, this is an opportunity for you to win a prize money and recognition. The theme of the contest is Nature in Nachod. Register for the competition with the registration fee, winner and runner up gets prize money and the photos will be published on the website with Photographer details and with the consent of owner.
